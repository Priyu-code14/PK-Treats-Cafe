About us

Nehru Institute of Engineering and Technology (NIET), Coimbatore, is a premier self-financing engineering college established in 2006, affiliated to Anna University, and approved by AICTE. It is recognized for its strong academic programs, industry tie-ups, and consistent placement record with top companies.

📌 Key Highlights of NIET

Establishment: Founded in 2006 under the Nehru Group of Institutions.
Affiliation & Approval: Affiliated to Anna University, Chennai and approved by AICTE, New Delhi.

✨Accreditations

NAAC A+ with a CGPA of 3.33
NBA Accreditation for Aeronautical Engineering and Computer Science Engineering
Certified under ISO 9001:2015 and ISO 14001:2015.
Ranking: Secured 173rd rank in India Today Ranking 2023 and 137th place among Best Private Engineering Colleges in South India by The Week.

🎓 Academics & Programs

Offers Undergraduate (B.E./B.Tech) and Postgraduate (M.E./MBA/MCA) programs.
Specialized departments include Aeronautical Engineering, Computer Science, Artificial Intelligence & Data Science, Mechanical, Electronics & Communication, Electrical & Electronics, and Management Studies.
Focus on innovation, research, and entrepreneurship, with active participation in events like the Smart India Hackathon.

💼 Placements & Industry Connect

Total Placements: Over 3,575+ students placed in reputed companies.
Top Recruiters: Boeing, Robert Bosch, Infosys, Accenture, Tech Mahindra, IBM, Siemens, TCS, British Telecom, and Capgemini.
Highest Package: ₹18 LPA.
Industry Tie-ups: More than 100 MoUs with leading industries for research, internships, and training.

🏫 Campus & Facilities

Located at Nehru Gardens, Thirumalayampalayam, Coimbatore, Tamil Nadu – 641105.
Facilities include modern classrooms, laboratories, seminar halls, auditoriums, and a well-stocked library.
Strong emphasis on student development through clubs, cultural events, and technical symposiums.
