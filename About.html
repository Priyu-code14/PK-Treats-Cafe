Our Story

Welcome to PK Treats, your cozy neighborhood café where good food meets great vibes. We serve delicious snacks, refreshing beverages, creamy coffees, and delightful desserts made with quality ingredients and care.

At PK Treats, we believe a café is more than just a place to eat — it’s a place to relax, connect, and create memories with friends and family. Our warm atmosphere, friendly service, and affordable menu make us the perfect spot for casual hangouts and sweet cravings.
